## Connect with your peripheral device

LattePanda is compatible with a wide range of peripheral devices. You may connect any USB supported devices, such flash drives, mouse and keyboard, or a webcam to USB 3.0 and 2.0 ports. The SD card socket supports extra storage from a mini SD card. You may also connect the LattePanda to an external speaker device through its 3.5mm audio jack.
